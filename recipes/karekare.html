Kare Kare

Description

Kare Kare, my personal favorite, is a type of Filipino stew with a thick and rich peanut sauce. The traditional recipe uses ox tail, and sometimes ox tripe is used.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs oxtail
  • 1 piece small banana flower bud
  • 1 bundle pechay or bok choy
  • 1 bundle string beans
  • 4 pieces eggplants
  • 1 cup ground peanuts
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 34 Ounces water
  • 1/2 cup annatto seeds
  • 1/2 cup toasted ground rice
  • 1 tbsp garlic minced
  • 1 piece onion chopped
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a large pot, bring the water to a boil
  2. Put the oxtail followed by the onions and simmer for 2.5 to 3 hrs or until tender (35 minutes if using a pressure cooker)
  3. Once the meat is tender, add the ground peanuts, and coloring (water from the annatto seed mixture) and simmer for 5 to 7 minutes
  4. Add the toasted ground rice and simmer for 5 minutes
  5. On a separate pan, saute the garlic then add the banana flower, eggplant, and string beans, and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Transfer the cooked vegetables to the large pot (where the rest of the ingredients are)
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste
  8. Serve how with shrimp paste. Enjoy!
